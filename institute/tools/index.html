The catalog
  • gloxxai/maturity-assessment Live

    The 30-question AI-QA Readiness Self-Assessment, embedded on this site at /institute/assessment. Vanilla HTML/JS, drop-in, no framework required. The scoring algorithm and question set are MIT-licensed so you can fork it for internal team self-assessments.

    Use it →
  • gloxxai/release-gate Week of May 19

    Open-source AI release-gate checklist generator. Scaffolds a CI workflow that runs your eval suite, compares against a documented threshold, and blocks the merge on regression. Ships with templates for GitHub Actions, GitLab CI, and CircleCI.

    ETA: week of May 19, 2026
  • gloxxai/eval-template Q3 2026

    DeepEval scaffold with the structure we use on every retainer — golden-set loader, faithfulness/relevancy/refusal-correctness suites, baseline-tracking helper. Ships with the documentation pattern that gets a team from L2 to L3 in 90 days.

    ETA: Q3 2026
